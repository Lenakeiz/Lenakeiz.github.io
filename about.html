About


Andrea Castegnaro is an italian guy living in London.

He trained as a phycisist in Rome and just after completing his studies he followed his passion for software/game development.
 
The opportunity to take part of a one year master degree at Goldsmiths University of London helped him to refine his development skills with the relevant industry tools. Skills that helped him to start a career as a freelance game developer.

As a result of his increasing interest in gamification of digital health he started a PhD in neuroscience at UCL. He is currently doing his final year and thinking at the next excting project.
