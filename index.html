Listen-In

Listen-In is first of a kind in gamification of digital health. It s current aim is to provide around 100 hours of therapy to older people who have been diagnosed with aphasia. The current version successfully passed the clinical trial and it is planned to be release on the Android store by March 2019.

Alzheimer's research

My main research focus consist of designing/developing tasks that can help the diagnose of neurodegenerative diseases such as the Alzheimer's disease.
Our effort is in pushing the detection line well beyond the onset of the real disease, outmatching this way the standard neuropsychological test that are been used nowadays.
I use commercial VR technlogy for this specific development.
