Listen-In

Listen-In is first of a kind in gamification of digital health.

It s aim is to provide at least 100 hours of therapy to older people who have been diagnosed with aphasia, subsequently to a stroke.

The project is done in collaboration with the neurotherapeutics group at UCL and I am the lead developer responsible for the game.

In 2017/2018 the product successfully passed the clinical trial and a completely new version will be release on the Android store by March 2019.

In this video you can see an extract from the clinical trial version.

Research

My main research focus is the detection of early stages of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's.

For doing so I design/develop tasks tailored to those brain regions that are primarily involved in the disease spread.

The effort consist in pushing the detection line well beyond the onset of the real disease, outmatching this way the results obtained from the standard neuropsychological test and giving the possibility of providing a more efficient treatment.

I use commercial VR technlogy for this specific development. In this video you can see the PIT (Path Integration Task) which is one of the tasks I developed during my PhD.
